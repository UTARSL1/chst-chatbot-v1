# Scopus Publications Dashboard Workflow

This guide explains how to access and use the Scopus Publications Dashboard to analyze research output for LKC FES departments.

## 1. Access the Dashboard
1. Open the application sidebar.
2. Click on the **Workspace** tab (grid icon).
3. Expand the **Faculty Dashboard** section.
4. Click on **Faculty Publications**.

## 2. Select Data Source
1. **Faculty**: Select "LKC FES" (currently strictly for LKC FES).
2. **Department**: Select one of the available departments (e.g., "DMBE - Department of Mechanical and Biomedical Engineering").
   - *Note*: If you see an "Access Denied" message, you do not have permission to view this department. Contact the Chairperson.

## 3. Analyze Data by Year
1. Locate the **Select Years to Analyze** panel.
2. Check or uncheck the boxes for **2023**, **2024**, and **2025**.
3. All statistics and tables below will instantly update to reflect only the publications from the selected years.

## 4. View Individual Staff Performance
1. Ensure the **Individual Staff** tab is active.
2. Review the table showing all staff members in the department.
3. **Columns**:
   - **Status**: "Available" means Scopus data was successfully linked.
   - **Publications**: Shows the total count for your selected years.
4. The list is automatically sorted by publication count (highest to lowest).

## 5. View Department Statistics
1. Click on the **Department Overview** tab.
2. Review the summary cards:
   - **Total Staff**: Number of academic staff in the department.
   - **With Scopus Data**: How many staff have linked Scopus profiles.
   - **Total Publications**: Combined output of the department for the selected years.
   - **Average per Staff**: Department efficiency metric.
3. **Chart**: Observe the "Publications by Year" bar chart to see the trend over the selected period.

## 6. Access Management (Chairperson Only)
If you are logged in as a Chairperson:
1. Select the Department you want to manage.
2. Click the **Manage Access** button in the top header.
3. **To Add User**:
   - Enter their email (e.g., `staff@utar.edu.my`) in the input box.
   - Click **Add**. They will instantly gain access.
4. **To Remove User**:
   - Find their email in the list.
   - Click the trash icon to revoke access.
