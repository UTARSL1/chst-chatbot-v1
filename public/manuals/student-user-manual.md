## Getting Started

### Creating Your Student Account

1. **Use Your UTAR Email**
   - You MUST register with your UTAR student email (@1utar.my)
   - Non-UTAR emails will be registered as public users

2. **Get an Invitation Code**
   - Obtain an invitation code from your faculty/department
   - Codes are typically shared via email or notice boards
   - One code can be used by multiple students

3. **Registration Process**
   - Navigate to the registration page
   - Enter your UTAR email address
   - Create a secure password
   - Enter the invitation code
   - Fill in your full name

4. **Email Verification**
   - Check your UTAR email for verification link
   - Click to verify your email address
   - Your account will be activated immediately

5. **Login**
   - Use your UTAR email and password
   - Access the full student interface

The chat interface is your primary way to interact with the chatbot.

**Basic Usage:**
1. Type your question in the input box
2. Press Enter or click Send
3. Wait for the response

## Best Practices

### Asking Effective Questions

**‚úÖ DO:**
- Be specific about what you need
- Use proper names and titles
- Specify departments or faculties
- Ask follow-up questions for clarification

**‚ùå DON'T:**
- Ask overly vague questions
- Combine too many unrelated topics
- Use unclear abbreviations
- Expect the chatbot to guess context

### Example Comparisons

**Good:**
- "Who is the Head of Department of Mechatronics and Biomedical Engineering?"
- "Show me the top 15 Nature Index journals ranked by impact factor"
- "List all professors in the Department of Computer Science"

**Needs Improvement:**
- "Who's the head?" (Which department?)
- "Show me journals" (Which journals? What criteria?)
- "List professors" (Which department/faculty?)

### Getting the Most Out of Tools

**Staff Search:**
- Use acronyms for faster results (D3E, DMBE)
- Specify roles for targeted searches (Dean, Head, Professor)
- Include faculty for cross-department searches

**Nature Index:**
- Be specific about journal names
- Use country filters when needed
- Ask for rankings when comparing journals

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Access

**Q: I registered with my UTAR email but don't have student access. Why?**
A: Ensure you used an invitation code during registration. Contact your administrator if issues persist.

**Q: Can I use the chatbot on my phone?**
A: Yes! The interface is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices.

**Q: My invitation code isn't working. What should I do?**
A: Check if the code has expired or contact the person who provided it. Codes may have usage limits.

### Using Features

**Q: Why can't I access JCR Impact Factors?**
A: JCR Journal Metrics are available only to member and chairperson roles. Students have access to Nature Index data instead.

**Q: Can I download all documents?**
A: You can download public and student-level documents. Member and chairperson documents are restricted.

**Q: How do I know which tool the chatbot is using?**
A: The chatbot automatically selects the appropriate tool based on your question. You'll see indicators in the response.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The chatbot is slow to respond. Is this normal?**
A: Response times vary based on query complexity. Staff searches and journal lookups may take a few seconds.

**Q: I can't see my chat history.**
A: Ensure you're logged in with the same account. Clear your browser cache if issues persist.

**Q: Can I export my chat history?**
A: Currently, chat history is viewable only within the interface. Export functionality may be added in future updates.
